Persistence

Keeping my hard work mentality when taking on light and even heavy tasks. The best times to learn are the hardest times, what matters is what you take from those times and how you utilize it in your future.

While working on a group project within a group for a media arts project we had an issue. One of our group members barely attended group meetings and really didn't do any work at all. My other group members were freaking out because they didn't want to have the heavy workload that would come from this. I stood up and kept my mind clear with two things in mind, first one being that I will complete this project and earn an A. My second goal would be to mellow out my team and to stay focused. Our project required us to complete an advertisement video that would convince our class to purchase our service. Being that we were missing one person, I asked a friend if he would like to participate and he figured it would be fun. Long story short, on our day we presented we created a successful video in which the whole class loved it. I informed my professor about our group member that did not participate and went from there.
