Determination

Always keeping my mind free of negativity and giving up. When working in-group projects or on teams, things are never going to be perfect. You might feel like they are, but in reality they are not. Keeping my mind free has helped myself achieve tasks that originally I did not think were possible.

For example, below I have my personal pc that I built from scratch. I had to first research the parts needed for my build and also take into account which parts will be compatible with others. I learned most of this from my Computer Hardware class at the University of South Carolina. Funny thing about that is that rather than finishing my computer in class, I ended up taking it home and finished it there instead. Being able to build my own PC sparked my interest like fire touching black powder. I would've never thought I would have the PC that I have today, it runs faster than any PC you can buy in the store
